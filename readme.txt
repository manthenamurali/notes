Easy Notes is a simple, light weight and elegant notepad application where you can take your notes down quickly. With the extremely easy navigation UI taking the notes is very easy. Save the notes to your own dropbox account so that the notes are only with you and secured.

Features:
Automatically saves the notes.
Assign labels to notes
Save notes in cloud to your dropbox account.
Share notes 
Save notes to SDCard
Create or delete new labels
Themes
Fonts
